Engagement Makeup FAQ

How early should I book my engagement makeup?

We recommend booking 2-3 months in advance, especially during peak wedding season. This ensures we can reserve your date and schedule a trial session.

Do you offer makeup trials?

Yes! We highly recommend a trial session. It's the perfect opportunity to discuss your vision, try different looks, and ensure we create your dream engagement look.

Can you match my makeup to my engagement outfit?

Absolutely! We'll coordinate your makeup with your outfit's color palette and style. Please share photos of your outfit during consultation.

What if I have sensitive skin?

We use hypoallergenic products suitable for sensitive skin. During your trial, we'll test products to ensure compatibility and comfort.

Do you travel to engagement venues?

Yes! We provide on-location services across Odisha. Travel fees may apply for venues outside Therubali, Rayagada.
