Bridal Makeup FAQ

How far in advance should I book my bridal makeup?

We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance, especially during peak wedding season, to secure your preferred date.

Do you offer trials for bridal makeup?

Yes! A pre-wedding trial is included in our Royal VIP package. For Standard and HD packages, trials can be booked separately from our Add-Ons menu.

Can you accommodate large bridal parties?

Absolutely! We have a team of experienced artists to handle large groups. Please check our Party Makeup page for group booking details or contact us directly.

What if I have sensitive skin?

We use high-quality, hypoallergenic products. Please inform us in advance about any sensitivities, and we'll do a patch test if needed.

Do you travel to different locations?

Yes, we travel across Odisha including Rayagada, Koraput, and Bhubaneswar. Travel fees may apply for locations outside Therubali.

What is included in the bridal makeup packages?

All our packages include professional makeup, hair styling, and draping. The specific products and services vary by package - see details above.
