Nail Art FAQs

How long does gel nail art last?

Our gel nail art typically lasts 2-3 weeks with proper care. To extend the life of your gel nails, avoid using them as tools, wear gloves when doing household chores, and apply cuticle oil daily.

Can I get nail art on natural nails?

Absolutely! We specialize in creating beautiful designs on natural nails. Our technicians will assess your nail health and recommend the best approach to enhance your natural nails with art.

How long does a nail art session take?

Session times vary based on design complexity:

  • Simple designs: 45-60 minutes
  • Moderate designs: 60-90 minutes
  • Intricate/3D art: 90-120 minutes

Do you offer nail extensions?

Yes, we offer various extension options including acrylic, gel, dip powder, and press-on customizations. All extensions include complementary nail art design.

Can I bring my own design?

We love creating custom designs! Bring inspiration photos or sketches to your appointment. Our artists will discuss feasibility, time requirements, and any modifications needed.

How should I prepare for my appointment?

For best results:

  • Remove any existing nail polish
  • Avoid cutting or pushing back cuticles
  • Bring inspiration images
  • Allow extra time for intricate designs
