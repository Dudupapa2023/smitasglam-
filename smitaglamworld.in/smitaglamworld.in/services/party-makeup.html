Party Makeup FAQ

How long does party makeup take?

Solo party makeup takes about 45-60 minutes. For family bundles, we allocate around 2.5 hours total. Please book well before your event.

Can I get a makeup trial before my event?

Yes! We highly recommend trials for special events. We offer separate trial sessions where we can perfect your look before the main event.

Do you provide services at my location?

Absolutely! We offer home/hotel/venue services across Odisha. Travel fees may apply for locations outside Therubali, Rayagada.

What if I have sensitive skin?

We use hypoallergenic products suitable for sensitive skin. During your trial, we'll test products to ensure compatibility and comfort.

Can you accommodate group bookings larger than 3?

Yes! The Family Bundle is for 3, but we can handle large wedding guest groups. It is simply ₹2000 for each additional person.
