Mehndi Art FAQ

How long does mehndi take to apply?

Depending on the complexity, bridal mehndi typically takes 2-4 hours, while engagement or party mehndi takes 1-2 hours. We always arrive well in advance for your event.

How long should I leave the mehndi paste on?

For the darkest stain, leave the paste on for 6-8 hours. We provide aftercare instructions to help you achieve the perfect color.

Can I customize my mehndi design?

Absolutely! We'll discuss your preferences during booking and create a unique design that complements your outfit and personal style.

Is your henna paste safe for sensitive skin?

Yes, we use 100% natural henna with no chemicals or additives. If you have concerns, we can do a patch test before your event.

Do you provide touch-up services?

We offer complimentary touch-ups for bridal packages within 1 hour of application. For other services, touch-ups are available at a nominal fee.
