Portfolio FAQ

Can I request a specific makeup style from your portfolio?

Yes! We encourage brides to save photos from our portfolio or Instagram feed to show us during the consultation. We will tailor that specific look to complement your unique facial features and skin tone.

Are the photos in your portfolio edited or filtered?

We pride ourselves on showcasing real brides and real skin. While lighting is optimized for photography, we do not heavily blur or alter skin texture, ensuring you see the authentic quality of our HD makeup.

How often is the portfolio updated?

Our live Instagram feed section is updated automatically every time we post! We also update our curated gallery tabs seasonally to reflect the latest bridal trends in Odisha.

Do you recreate celebrity bridal looks?

Absolutely. If you have a celebrity inspiration in mind, share it with us. We use premium international brands (like MAC, Huda Beauty) to recreate those iconic, flawless finishes.
